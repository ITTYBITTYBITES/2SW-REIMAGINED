## Narrative secret

- **What appears to have happened:** The reactor console appears faulty, or the physicist’s marked calibration key appears to have caused a dangerous alignment failure.
- **Who is blamed:** The physicist is implicitly blamed for a mistake made at the console.
- **Hidden truth:** The console is receiving the future pressure of a reactor collapse early enough to warn her.
- **Why it matters:** The impossible reading is not evidence of failure. It is the fraction of time needed to prevent a citywide catastrophe.
