# Chapter 01 Content Depth Audit

This audit evaluates the narrative quality, lore depth, and mechanical clarity of Chapter 01: "The First Fractures" after upgrading all five core moments (`WM_001` - `WM_005`) from basic mechanics into highly immersive, emotionally resonant experiences.

---

## 1. Contextual Depth Upgrades

### 1.1. WM_001 — The Unfinished Canvas
- **Narrative Depth:** Establishes the painter’s profound hesitation and connection to Clara. Clara’s color note provides the exact prompt explaining his artistic waiting.
- **Evidence Node Upgrades:**
  - `paused_brush` -> `"The Suspended Brush"` (loaded with rare cobalt pigment, hanging in the air).
  - `crystal_prism` -> `"The Wife's Crystal Prism"` (quartz crystal belonging to Clara, splitting light).
  - `color_notes` -> `"Clara's Color Note"` (Clara's handwriting, waiting for the 5:14 PM sun).
- **Richer Resolution:** Explicitly focuses on the painter’s longing, wait for light, and final glimpse of Clara’s presence.

### 1.2. WM_002 — The Forgotten Museum
- **Narrative Depth:** Focuses on Arthur's forty years of quiet, daily museum corridor protection as a tribute to his grandfather's fossil.
