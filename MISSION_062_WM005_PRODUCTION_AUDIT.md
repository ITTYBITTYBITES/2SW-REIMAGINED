## Narrative secret

- **What appears to have happened:** A biological/instrumental reflection has begun to move independently of the observer.
- **Hidden truth:** The Iris is already looking back through the same aperture; the player is not outside the system.
- **Why the memory matters:** Damaged memories persist only when an external witness returns attention to them. The player’s act of observation is part of the Iris’s preservation process.
- **Larger mystery:** The Iris has been holding the door open for a witness capable of restoring what it cannot preserve alone.

## Production decision

Extend only `wm_005.json` and the existing Iris dialogue registry. No runtime, routing, Archive, persistence, navigation, progression, chapter, or gameplay-system change is required.

The authored recovered truth is **Shared Aperture**: Witness and Iris preserve damaged memories together by sharing attention.
