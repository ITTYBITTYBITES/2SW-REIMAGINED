## Existing narrative secret

- **Initial belief:** Elena is escaping a dangerous homeland; the early telegram and opening case make her departure feel inevitable and frightening.
- **Hidden truth:** Her final music and her brother’s safety have already crossed the ocean, transforming the journey into a reunion.
- **Why it matters:** The memory is not about losing a homeland; it is about finding the courage to leave because a safe harbor is already waiting.

The authored recovered truth is **Safe Harbor**: Elena’s final note had already opened a safe path to her brother across the sea.
