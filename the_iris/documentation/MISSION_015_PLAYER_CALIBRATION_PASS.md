## 2. Target vs. Unintended Mental Models

The primary discovery of MISSION 015 is identifying which mental model players leave with:

### Intended Model (Success)
> *"I am examining a memory, reconstructing what matters, and using perspectives to understand what changed."*

### Unintended Model (Failure)
> *"I am searching for the developer's hidden trick / guessing what changed."*

## 3. Anti-Bias Facilitator Rules

Before the session, facilitators must strictly adhere to the following rules:
- Do not mention "false details."
- Do not describe it as a memory test.
- Do not explain attunements.
- Do not demonstrate the reconstruction system.

During the session, record behavioral markers verbatim:
1. First action taken
2. First verbal assumption
3. First confusion point
4. First successful insight

## 4. Observation Capture Questionnaire & Retention Signal

After completion, collect:
1. *"What do you think you were supposed to do?"*
2. *"What moment were you trying to remember?"*
3. *"What made you choose your answer?"*

**Retention Signal (Curiosity Persistence):**
