## 1. Overall Assessment

Chapter 1 (*"Learning to Notice"*) has successfully transitioned from a technical proof-of-concept into a cohesive, multi-faceted cognitive journey. Across four distinct scenarios (WM_001 through WM_004), the player is guided through progressively deeper domains of human experience—from private artistic inspiration to institutional legacy, intimate musical dedication, and high-stakes scientific precision. 

The review confirms that the chapter does not feel like a collection of random puzzles; rather, it forms a deliberate curriculum teaching the player the complete language of Two Second Witness.

---

## 2. Moment-by-Moment Narrative Progression

### WM_001 — The Unfinished Canvas (Personal / Light)
- **Teaching Focus:** Establishes the foundational act of *presence and perception*. The player learns that observation is about noticing beauty and inspiration (the crystal prism splitting golden light) rather than hunting for mistakes.

### WM_002 — The Forgotten Museum (Collective / Legacy)
- **Teaching Focus:** Expands observation into *historical continuity*. The player connects personal habit (the guard's palm imprint on the glass case) to generational heritage (his grandfather's ammonite donation).

### WM_003 — The Last Performance (Emotional / Intention)
- **Teaching Focus:** Deepens observation into *artistic intention and emotional resonance*. The player perceives that a performance is shaped not by auditorium applause, but by a telegram sent across the sea to Elena.

### WM_004 — The Faulty Reactor (Precision / Consequence)
- **Teaching Focus:** Challenges observation with *scientific consequence*. The player discovers that precise attention to micro-variances (a 0.2mm laser deflection on a quartz sensor) is the boundary between system stability and catastrophe.

---

## 3. Difficulty Curve & Cognitive Load

- **Baseline 1 (Introductory):** All four moments maintain an accessible baseline difficulty, ensuring cognitive load remains focused on comprehension rather than mechanical complexity.
- **Curve Progression:**
  - *WM_001:* Teaches the core loop (Observe → Reconstruct → Investigate → Reveal).
  - *WM_002:* Expands spatial fragment placement and historical text/thermal attunements.
  - *WM_003:* Deepens emotional context and forensic/spectral attribution.
  - *WM_004:* Challenges technical observation and multi-variable sensor correlation.

---

## 4. Witness Grammar Compliance Review

A rigorous audit against `WITNESS_GRAMMAR.md` confirms **zero drift**:
- **Observation:** Every moment strictly enforces the 2.0-second locked cinematic window, fostering calm reflection over panic.
- **Reconstruction:** Fragment palettes serve exclusively as memory anchors, avoiding inventory clutter or arbitrary puzzle mechanics.
- **Investigation:** Attunements across all moments (spectral, forensic, text, thermal, trajectory) function as discovery tools revealing multi-perspective layers of truth.
- **Revelation:** Dynamic archive entry assembly provides emotional closure and Iris reflection without arbitrary scoring.
- **Failure / Grace:** Zero punishment loops; complete moments return gracefully to Iris via the established grace window.

## 6. WM_005 Design Requirements (Final Chapter Moment)

To make the entire chapter feel complete, WM_005 (*The Witness*) must fulfill the following architectural and narrative criteria:

- **Required Emotional Purpose:** **Convergence and Origin.** The player realizes that the memories they have been examining are not isolated historical events, but fragments of the Iris's own awakening.
- **Required Difficulty Level:** Baseline 1 / Culminating Synthesis.
- **Required Mechanical Emphasis:** Harmonious synthesis of all prior attunement types (spectral, forensic, thermal, trajectory, text) into a single reflective memory.
- **Relationship to Previous Entries:** Acts as the keystone archive entry that ties together art (WM_001), history (WM_002), art/music (WM_003), and science (WM_004) into a unified narrative thread.
