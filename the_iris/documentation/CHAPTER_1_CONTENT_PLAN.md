## 1. Chapter Theme & Narrative Arc

Chapter 1 is titled **"Learning to Notice"**. Its purpose is to teach the player the foundational grammar of Two Second Witness through progressively resonant spaces, moving from private intimacy to public memory.

### Narrative Progression (WM_001 — WM_005)
1. **WM_001 — The Unfinished Canvas:** Private space, golden hour. Learning to notice *inspiration and light* rather than mistakes.
2. **WM_002 — The Forgotten Museum:** Public/institutional space, night archive. Learning to notice *generational heritage and human connection* to history.
3. **WM_003 — The Last Performance:** Liminal space, abandoned theater. Learning to notice *lingering emotional resonance* and performance echoes.
4. **WM_004 — The Faulty Reactor:** Industrial space, control room. Learning to notice *functional anomalies and human sacrifice*.
5. **WM_005 — The Witness:** Threshold space, the core architecture. Synthesizing all previous lessons into the mystery of the Iris.
