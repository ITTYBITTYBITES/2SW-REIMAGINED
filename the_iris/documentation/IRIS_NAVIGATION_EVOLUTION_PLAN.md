# Iris Navigation Evolution Plan

## Current foundation

The current Iris already owns:

- center activation;
- directional destination cues;
- optical entry/return transitions;
- Android Back;
- input intent abstraction;
- orientation/device adaptation;
- voice, visual, haptic, caption guidance;
- desktop Mobile Simulator.

## Evolution path

### Phase A — comprehension foundation

Current phase:

- center opens Story Mode placeholder;
- Discover reveals Daily Witness, Weekly Investigation, Archive, Your Iris, and Calibration signals;
- placeholder destinations show the future ecosystem without adding gameplay;
- existing production rooms remain behind destination hosts.

### Phase B — guided selection

Future:

- Iris/Director identifies the most relevant next experience;
- Discover points gain remembered context;
- Daily and Weekly state comes from local production data;
- center Story Mode resumes the current Rank Chapter.

### Phase C — narrative continuity

Future:

- Story Chapter beat begins before the internal family is exposed;
- Witness Moment entry uses the Iris transition;
- result/evidence returns as a memory response;
- Archive updates from production history.

### Phase D — mastery depth

Future:

- Your Iris unifies profile, rank, mastery, discoveries, records, achievements, and calibration;
- Library remains the explicit advanced path;
- current production routes remain accessible through accessibility/deep-link/recovery paths.

## Invariants

- Left always means Archive/Library.
- Right always means Discover.
- Down always means Your Iris/Profile.
- Up always means Calibration/Settings.
- Center always means Story Mode/Witness entry.
- Back always returns to the Iris before exiting or changing to another destination.

## Do not do

- Do not add a tab bar to the Iris.
- Do not turn the Discover constellation into a card grid.
- Do not make challenge families the primary destination names.
- Do not duplicate NavigationService or production save systems.
- Do not bypass ChallengeSessionService when a future point launches gameplay.
