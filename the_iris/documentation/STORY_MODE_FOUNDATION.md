# Story Mode Foundation

## Purpose

Story Mode is the primary journey of Two Second Witness 4.0. It turns existing challenge mechanics into internal sequences inside a coherent Witness Moment.

```text
Iris
→ Story Mode
→ Rank Chapter
→ Witness Moment
→ Challenge Sequence
→ Evidence Reveal
→ Reward
→ Archive Update
→ Return to Iris
```

## Placeholder chapter structure

### Rank 1 — Observer

Fantasy: learning to stay with a field.

Theme: quiet attention.

Example moment: a room where one meaningful detail is easy to miss.

### Rank 2 — Investigator

Fantasy: comparing and questioning what changed.

Theme: evidence and cause.

Example moment: two states of a scene with a subtle change.

### Rank 3 — Archivist

Fantasy: holding connections over time.

Theme: memory, pattern, and accumulated witness.

Example moment: a sequence or collection whose relationships matter.

## Witness Moment contract

A future moment should provide:

- moment ID and chapter ID;
- framing language/voice;
- challenge sequence list;
- production family/template references;
- difficulty/Director context;
- evidence/reveal references;
- reward/progression declaration;
- return-memory response;
- resume/interruption state.

## Director boundary

The Story Mode Director selects a moment and calls production services. It does not implement generation, scoring, save, or family mechanics.

## Current placeholder behavior

The Story Mode placeholder is intentionally lightweight. It exposes Rank 1 / Observer, attention progress, and a focus point that opens the current production Witness flow. The placeholder proves the future hierarchy without pretending that Story Chapters are complete.

## Completion definition for future implementation

A first Story Mode implementation is ready when:

- the Iris opens a named chapter/moment;
- observation, recall, evidence, reward, and return all form one uninterrupted experience;
- production progress remains authoritative;
- the player can stop and resume safely;
- accessibility variants remain equivalent;
- the next moment is guided by history, mastery, and variety rather than random family selection.
