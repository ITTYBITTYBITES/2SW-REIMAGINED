## Coverage balance

The roadmap intentionally balances:

- history and archives;
- science and engineering;
- art and restoration;
- mystery without default crime/horror framing;
- exploration and natural phenomena;
- everyday human moments;
- empathy and reflection;
- impossible but emotionally grounded futures.

These are production directions, not full moment designs. Each requires the Master Template, accessibility review, evidence contract, content validation, and human playtest before implementation.
