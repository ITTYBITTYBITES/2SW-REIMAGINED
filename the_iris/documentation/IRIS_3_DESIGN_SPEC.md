# IRIS 3.0 DESIGN SPECIFICATION — Living Cognitive Instrument

**Campaign:** Architecture Convergence  
**Phase:** Phase 13 — Living Iris 3.0 Flagship Redesign  
**Status:** Approved & Implemented  
**Date:** 2026-07-17  
**Author:** Director Agent (Arena.ai)

---

## 1. Design Vision & Concept

The **Living Iris 3.0** redesign transitions Two Second Witness from an earlier visual iteration (Iris 2.5) into a flagship product identity. Iris is no longer a simple eyeball graphic or static menu shell; she is a **living cognitive instrument**—a scientific and artistic gateway into memory, combining bioluminescent deep-space optics, crystalline stroma fibers, and orbital memory shard constellations that respond directly to player progression.

### Core Identity Statement
> *"An instrument that observes and preserves moments."*

---

## 2. Flagship Visual States

### 1. Dormant (First Arrival)
- **Visuals:** Subdued seafoam and emerald limbal glow, slow rhythmic breathing (48 Hz sub-bass cadence), gentle ambient dust motes swirling in the cornea.
- **Purpose:** Elicit curiosity, mystery, and a quiet invitation to interact.

### 2. Awakening (Interaction Triggered)
- **Visuals:** Saccadic biological eye tracking, radial fiber expansion, responsive gaze anticipation, and subtle haptic pulsing.
- **Purpose:** Establish that the Iris recognizes the player and that the cognitive instrument is active.

### 3. Witness Entry (Transition into Memory)
- **Visuals:** Pupil aperture dilates smoothly, portal destination preview expands, spatial distortion waves warp the cornea as the player is drawn through the optical stroma.
- **Purpose:** Forge an unbreakable perceptual connection between the Living Iris and the memory case.

### 4. Reflection (Post-Witness Completion)
- **Visuals:** Orbital memory shards (WM_001 through WM_005) illuminate brightly around the collarette, stroma warmth scales with Insight accumulation, and completed archive entries reflect in the cornea.
- **Purpose:** Visually communicate learning, progression growth, and archive permanence.

---

## 3. Technical Integration & Contracts

- **Authority Preservation:** Retains existing `IrisController` parameter control (`pupil_dilation`, `glow_strength`, `progression_level`, `gaze_target`) and singular progression authority (`PlayerProgressService`).
- **Zero Runtime Interference:** Witnesses, Incident Registry, and Chapter 1 content remain untouched. Iris 3.0 is a pure, high-fidelity presentation upgrade.
