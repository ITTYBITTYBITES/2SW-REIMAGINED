# Chapter One — Learning to Notice

**Rank:** Observer
**Theme:** “I Notice”
**Purpose:** Teach the player that attention is an active, compassionate, and rewarding way of moving through the world.

## Chapter arc

Chapter One begins with ordinary spaces and gradually teaches the player to notice presence, absence, time, relationship, and the Iris itself. The chapter should not feel like ten tutorials. Each moment introduces one new emotional or perceptual shade while preserving a calm shared vocabulary.

```text
Invitation
→ presence
→ absence
→ time
→ relationship
→ perspective
→ memory
→ first evidence chain
→ first Archive meaning
→ Iris relationship
```

## Moment 1 — The Forgotten Museum

### Arrival

The Iris opens into a quiet gallery. A single room is visible, with one display that seems ordinary but carries a quiet visual tension.

### Attunement

The player receives the intention:

> “Stay with the room.”

No family name or puzzle instruction is given.

### Observation

The player observes one fair, bounded field. The moment should establish that the player’s attention is the primary action.

### Memory / investigation

The player reconstructs one relationship or absence. The investigation gently directs attention to the relevant evidence.

### Revelation

The display’s overlooked detail becomes legible. The player understands why it mattered.

### Reward / return

First Discovery is stored. Observer progress increases. The Iris remembers the object as a quiet rim response.

## Moment 2 — The Window Across the Street

Teaches that observation can include human presence without requiring the player to judge or spy. A repeated light or gesture changes the interpretation of the window.

## Moment 3 — The Cup That Stayed Warm

Teaches that small physical traces imply care. The player reconstructs a recent action from steam, placement, and a missing companion object.

## Moment 4 — The Garden Bench

Teaches absence. The player notices what is no longer there and investigates without turning absence into a crime mystery.

## Moment 5 — A Note in the Margin

Teaches incomplete evidence. The player connects a notation to an object or place and learns that the Archive can preserve context.

## Moment 6 — The Last Train Light

Introduces time and anticipation. The player observes a repeating signal and understands a single interruption.

## Moment 7 — The Shape of Dust

Introduces environmental traces. The player discovers that a surface can record movement without showing the movement itself.

## Moment 8 — The Unfinished Photograph

Introduces incomplete memory and reflection. The player is allowed uncertainty and does not need to force a definitive emotional answer.

## Moment 9 — The Quiet Repair

Introduces evidence chains. The player connects tools, material, and a repaired object to understand an act of care.

## Moment 10 — The Iris Opens

Closes the chapter by making the instrument itself part of the story. The player recognizes that the Iris has been learning their attention and that future chapters will build on this relationship.

## Why this order teaches well

1. **Presence before absence:** the player first learns to look at what exists.
2. **Ordinary before symbolic:** meaning is earned through familiar places.
3. **Single detail before relationships:** attention becomes more complex gradually.
4. **Human warmth before mystery:** the chapter avoids fear as the default engagement tool.
5. **Uncertainty before mastery:** the player learns that not every moment needs a perfect answer.
6. **Evidence chains before chapter conclusion:** the player is ready to see the Iris as a memory instrument.

## Gameplay progression

- Moments 1–2: one observation field, one discovery.
- Moments 3–4: presence/absence and simple reconstruction.
- Moments 5–6: evidence relationships and time.
- Moments 7–8: environmental/fragmented memory.
- Moments 9–10: connected evidence and reflection.

Exact mechanics remain TBD and must be selected by the future Witness Mechanics Framework.

## Investigation progression

- Observe one field.
- Follow one cue.
- Locate one trace.
- Connect two details.
- Interpret an incomplete record.
- Preserve a meaningful discovery.

## Rank progression

Production XP/mastery remains authoritative. Chapter One’s player-facing Rank progression frames the first ten moments as movement from “I can notice” to “I understand why it matters.”
